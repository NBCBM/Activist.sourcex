Best websites to get unlimited test and assignment for school practice

There are many websites that offer unlimited tests and assignments for school practice. Here are a few:

Khan Academy: Khan Academy is a free educational website that offers practice exercises, instructional videos, and personalized learning dashboard for students in many subjects, including math, science, and humanities.

Quizlet: Quizlet is a free online study tool that allows students to create and share flashcards, quizzes, and games for learning.

Edmodo: Edmodo is an online learning platform that provides a safe and easy way for students and teachers to connect and collaborate, and it also offers a wide range of educational resources, including practice tests and assignments.

Study Island: Study Island is an online learning platform that offers a variety of interactive practice tests and assignments for K-12 students in math, science, and language arts.

IXL Learning: IXL Learning is a comprehensive learning platform that provides interactive practice tests and assignments for students in math, science, language arts, and social studies.

Keep in mind that some of these websites may require a subscription or payment for full access to their resources.
