How to get submit toefl scores in common app

How to send official scores

To submit official TOEFL scores to Common App, you will need to request that ETS (the organization that administers the TOEFL) send your scores directly to the colleges you are applying to. Follow these steps:

Log in to your ETS account on the TOEFL website.

Click on the "View Scores and Score Recipients" option.

Find the college(s) you want to send your scores to and click on the "Send Additional Score Reports" button.

Enter the necessary information, including the college(s) and any required recipient codes.

Pay the fee for each score report you request.

Your official TOEFL scores will be sent directly to the colleges you selected.

Note that it may take several business days for the scores to be processed and sent, so be sure to submit your request well in advance of any application deadlines. Additionally, be sure to check the specific requirements for each college, as some may have additional instructions or requirements for submitting TOEFL scores.

How to self report the scores

To submit TOEFL scores in Common App, follow these steps:

Log in to your Common App account.

Go to the "My Colleges" tab and select the college to which you want to submit your TOEFL scores.

Under the "Testing" section, click on the "Add Test" button.

Select "TOEFL" from the drop-down menu and enter your test scores and the date you took the test.

Save your information and repeat the process for any additional colleges you want to send your TOEFL scores to.
